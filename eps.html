Student Team Participants

The Engineering Project Showcase event is designed to celebrate the amazing work of more than 1,000 engineering students in senior capstone design courses. 

Students represent several engineering majors including: AERO, BAEN, BMEN, CHEN, CSCE, ECEN, ETID, ISEN, ITDE, MEEN, and MSEN.

 

Industry Judges

Industry representatives are invited to join us in-person as judges.  See the innovative approaches student teams have utilized to address real-world problems, provide them valuable feedback, and helps us recognize this year's top teams. 

Furthermore, observe examples of how industry and others leverage capstone projects to get value for their orgnaization. 

 

Showcase Goals

Industry Engagement: Raise awareness about the value of sponsored capstone projects to industry and others.

Retention: Support retention efforts by showing freshman and sophomores examples of real-world capstone projects.

Recruitement: Raise STEM awareness with prospective students and teachers.

 

Sponsorship Opportunities

The Engineering Project Showcase offers a great opportunity for industry to raise awareness about their organization, support engineering innovation, and recognize top teams within majors of interest to their organization.

Join us and celebrate the innovators of tomorrow! Sponsorship opportunities start at $2.5k.

For more information email us at inp@tamu.edu.
